Welcome

Hope for Paws is a non-profit organisation dedicated to rescuing and rehoming abandoned animals. Join us in making a difference.

Hope for Paws Animal Shelter was established in 2015 by a group of animal welfare volunteers who were passionate about addressing the growing issue of stray and neglected animals. Starting as a small rescue initiative, the organisation has grown into a recognised non-profit that provides shelter, medical care, and adoption services. Through donations and volunteer support, the shelter continues to positively impact the lives of animals and the community.


Our Mission

At Hope For Paws, our mission is to rescue, protect and provide compassionate care to abandoned, abused and homeless animals. We are dedicated to offering safe shelter, medical treatment and rehabilitation while working tirelessly to find loving, permanent homes. Through community outreach and education, we strive to promote responsible pet ownership and create a more humane world for us and all animals in it.

In addition to the above, we are committed to building a compassionate community that values and respects animal life. We collaborate with volunteers and vets to expand our impact, ensuring that every animal receives the care it deserves. By raising awareness and advocating for animal welfare, our aim is to reduce neglect and create lasting change for future generations to come.

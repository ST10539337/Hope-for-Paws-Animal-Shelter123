Founder



Oamogetswe was driven by a deep compassion for animals and a desire to make a real difference in their lives. After witnessing the harsh conditions faced by abandoned and neglected animals, she felt a strong responsibility to take action. Volunteering with other rescue groups exposed her to the growing need for dedicated resources, funding and hand on care. Over time it became clear that starting an independent organisation would allow her to focus on the most urgent and challenging rescue cases.

Hope For Paws was created to provide a safe haven for animals that had nowhere else to turn to. Oamogetswe wanted to ensure that every rescued animal received not only immediate attention but also long term care and the chance to find a loving home. By building a network of supporters, volunteers, and partner organisation, she turned a personal passion into a mission that continues to save and transform the lives of countless animals.

# Hope-for-Paws-Animal-Shelter123
Hope for Paw Animal Shelter is a compassionate, community-driven non-profit organisation dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned, abused, and neglected animals. Founded on the belief that every animal deserves safety, dignity, and love, the organisation serves as a refuge for vulnerable animals while actively working to reduce the cycle of homelessness through responsible adoption and education.

At the heart of Hope for Paw is a commitment to holistic care. Each animal that enters the shelter receives medical attention, proper nutrition, and emotional rehabilitation to restore trust and wellbeing. Beyond rescue efforts, the organisation plays a vital role in community upliftment by promoting awareness around animal welfare, responsible pet ownership, and the importance of sterilisation.

What sets Hope for Paw apart is its people-centered approach—recognising that lasting change comes from empowering communities as much as protecting animals. Through outreach initiatives, volunteer programmes, and partnerships, the shelter fosters a culture of empathy and accountability.

Hope for Paw Animal Shelter is more than a safe haven; it is a movement rooted in kindness, aiming to create a future where no animal is left behind and every paw finds its way home.

# Hope for Paws Animal Shelter Website

## Project Overview

Hope for Paws is a responsive animal shelter website designed to promote animal welfare, encourage pet adoption, and provide information about the shelter and its services. The website aims to create awareness about rescued animals and encourage community involvement through volunteering and donations.

This project was developed as part of my Web Development Portfolio of Evidence (POE). Throughout the project, I progressively applied HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design principles, and version control using GitHub to create a functional and user-friendly website.

---

## Project Objectives

* Promote pet adoption and responsible pet ownership.
* Raise awareness about animal welfare.
* Provide information about the shelter and available pets.
* Encourage volunteering and donations.
* Create an attractive, interactive, and responsive website.
* Apply web development concepts and best practices.
